Fundraising

Your Gilbert Band Booster board provides as many opportunities as possible to raise funds to help cover the costs of the marching band, winter guard, and winter drumline programs. Fundraisers and dates subject to change. Please always read your emails from the boosters for current fundraising information. For more information regarding current fundraisers, please contact: Destiny McGaughy, Fundraising Chair GBBFundraiser@gmail.com

There are three kinds of fundraisers:

Individual Fundraisers

Funds raised go directly toward a student’s fees.

2019-20 General Fundraisers

Funds raised help to cover general band expenses. Remember – your band fees only cover a percentage of the total cost of running our award-winning programs. All students are expected to participate in general fundraisers to help keep fees as low as possible.

Trip Fundraisers

Certain fundraisers will be designated for trips – most frequently, the annual marching band trip to California and the “big trips” held about every three years.

General Fundraisers

Sponsor Packages

Black Package includes a 44' x 26' sponsorship logo on our trailer that follows us to all away games and competitions, advertisement in all band programs and website advertisement. This package sells for $900 and the students receive $400 towards fees.

Gold Package includes advertisement in all band programs and website advertisement. This package sells for $250 and the students receive $125 towards fees.

Business may add a banner for $100 to be displayed at Band Functions.

Sponsorships are a general AND individual fundraiser, earning money both for the band and for individual fees.
