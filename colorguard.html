Color Guard

The Gilbert High School (GHS) Color Guard is a non-musical section of our program that provides additional visual aspects to the marching bands performance. They perform alongside the marching band at football games and most guards regularly compete in competitions during the fall. The purpose of the color guard is to interpret the music that the marching band or drum and bugle corps is playing via the synchronized spinning of flags, sabers, rifles, the air blade, and through dance.

In addition to marching band, they also perform during the Winter Season.
