Signing up to Volunteer in CutTime
Step 1: Sign in to your parent or student portal
Step 2: Look on the right side under Volunteer opportunities (Picture 1 - blue circle) and click Details.
This opens the Available opportunities screen. (Picture 2)
 
 
Step 3: Review the Event, Activity, Date, Start Time, Shift Duration, and Notes about the volunteer opportunity.
Step 4: When you find an opportunity that works for you, click I’m Interested
(Picture 2 - blue circle)
Step 5: Once you have been selected to volunteer, it will show up in your parent/student portal page under “My volunteer activities…”
(Picture 1 - green circle)
